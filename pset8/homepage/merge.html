Merge Sort

Merge sorting aims to order an array or list by dividing it into two halves. The left half will be sorted before the right half, where each half will then be divided into two other halves. This process is repeated until we have two halves of size 1. Once each half is ordered, the left and right halves are merged back together.

Steps to Merge Sorting

Step 1

Find the middle point of the array or list and divide the list into two halves.

Step 2

Call the merge function to sort the left half of the split list

Step 3

Call the merge function to sort the right half of the split list

Step 4

Merge the sorted halves together. Merge sort has a logarithmic time complexity
